# RF-02 - Forum and Posts

## Goal
Enable users to publish and interact with social content in the Paw Meet community.

## Scope
- Publish text posts.
- Publish image posts.
- Comment on posts.
- React to posts with likes.
- Filter/search posts by topic, category, or popularity.
- Moderate inappropriate posts (authorized roles only).

## Acceptance Criteria
- Logged-in users can create text and image posts.
- Users can comment on existing posts.
- Users can like posts and likes are reflected in UI/data.
- Forum supports filtering/searching by at least topic and popularity.
- Unauthorized users cannot execute moderation actions.
